BorgaGarden

Borgagården has been an inn since 1948 when Linnea Näslund built the first part of the main building, which today is used for our fine dining restaurant & saloon pub

Fine-Dining

The restaurant, with a captivating panorama out over Borgahällan, Borgasjön and the mountains. Here you eat well made food, made from locally hand picked ingredients.
Must be booked 8PM the day before

Saloon Pub

The saloon, with a wild west theme, Borgafjäll's mountain chain right outside the sheriffs door. Here you have a good variaty of both big and local beers, lay back and relax in front of our open fireplace.
Drop-in from 5PM everyday
